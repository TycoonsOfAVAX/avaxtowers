About AVAX Towers

Getting Started

AVAX Tower is a city themed play to earn game featuring a real estate simulation. Every item in the game is a collectible ERC-721 token and can be traded on the open market.

Core Game Assets

  • Snow Globe Collection
  • City Buildings Collection
  • Towers Collection
  • Room Collection
  • $RENT Token

The game's multiple token collections interact with one another to create a vibrant real estate simulation for players to build the real estate empires upon.

Ways to play

Tower city is growing with new residents moving in every day. There are many ways to make your mark based on your invovlement level and personal perference.

  • Collect and rent out buildings & rooms (stake) to NPC residents
  • Provide services to NPC residents (Food, entertainment and services)
  • Provide citywide services to players (Utilities & emergency services)
  • Trade properteries on the open market
  • Produce materials for other players (Industries Addon)
  • Collect taxes from other players
  • Govern a city and set policies (tax rate, DAO budget spend and etc...)

Frequently Asked Questions

The team behind AVAX Towers believes in building a strong foundation before building up. We are on a mission to build a game that will last for the long haul and not here to fork or clone the latest fad game out there. Everything about the game will be built from the ground up. Do check our discord and twitter to see the latest developments in the game.

Note that AVAX Towers NFT series is first and foremost artistic NFT collections and not an investment or security of any form. Players who participate in the real estate simulation do so because they love the NFT arts and want to be part of the simulation that goes with it. One should not be expecting to make any returns from this game.

For starters, we cannot promise guaranteed payouts as this is foremost an artistic project with an economic simulation as an experiment, not an investment/security.
Unlike other projects, our mission is to gamify the defi space by putting a % of presale funds into trusted defi projects such as AAVE and Curve. Players are encouraged to use the in game tokens to grow their real estate empires and experience the game to the fullest.
Note that the DAO fund and simulation rewards will vary depending on market condition and are exposed to different types of risks from security risks on AAVE/Curve, liquidation from volatility and many more. The team has decided to diversify across different platforms to minimize risks that could affect monthly Payouts.

AVAX Tower is designed with a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) in mind. Players will be forming a city council where they will vote on how to use the monthly yield from DEFI activities. This includes sweeping up floor, buy and burn $RENT tokens, compounding to blue chips, spending on higher risk plays (majority votes required) or allocating to marketing & community activities.

Players are encouraged to participate in periodical elections as indepedent or as a party. Candidates will campaign players to commit tokens (for burning) to a 'ballot contract' in the candidate's name. Highest number of tokens burnt determines the winners of the election. Some seats are 'appointed' seats where the holder of a City Hall NFT gains the seat. Elected seats are equal or greater than appointed seats to ensure fairness.

We've played all sorts of city games from Sim City (OG versions) all the way to Cities skylines and niche versions like Workers & Resources: Soviet Republic and Caesar. We can comfortably say we've put in thousands of hours buidling cities here and there. We draw on those games as inspiration for a number of game mechanics. You do not need to be a city gamer to do well in this game. But if you are, you'll be in for a lot of nice references ;).
